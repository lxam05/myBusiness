What we do

Late Visuals, founded in 2025 by college students, helps the elderly and sick transition from home to hospital by recreating their favorite room, making the hospital feel safe and familiar.

→

How we do it

By capturing precise photos of a customer’s room, we use 3D mapping to project the images onto hospital curtains, helping patients feel at home during a difficult time.

→

Our inspiration

The idea came to me after seeing a hospital full of sick, lonely people. Their lives were already changed by illness, yet being taken from the comfort of their homes into a cold, unfamiliar place made it even harder.
