How We Do It

Step 1: Capture and Consult

Our first step is to take precise measurements of the space, ensuring that every detail is accounted for—whether it’s a small picture frame or a large piece of furniture positioned against the wall. Nothing is overlooked, as accuracy at this stage is essential for a seamless result. Once the measurements and images are collected, we transfer them to our system. This is where the creative and technical work truly begins, transforming the captured details into the foundation of the project.

Step 2: Digital Recreation

Following the high-precision capture of the spatial dimensions surrounding the patient's bed, the process advances to the digital integration stage. Within a specialized 3D rendering environment, the source image undergoes a series of geometric transformations—including cropping, scaling, and perspective correction. These meticulous adjustments are performed to ensure the image seamlessly aligns with the physical contours of the room, thereby creating a realistic and immersive simulation of the patient's home environment.

Step 3: Installation & Immersion

Using our state-of-the-art projectors, we ensure that every image displayed is sharp, vibrant, and crystal clear. By utilizing the wall behind the bed as the projection base, we extend the visuals seamlessly across the three opposite walls surrounding the patient. This creates a warm, immersive environment that feels familiar and homely, helping to promote comfort and relaxation. The installation process is designed to be quick, smooth, and minimally invasive. Our team works efficiently to set everything up with as little disruption as possible, ensuring the space remains calm and undisturbed while we transform the room.
